Help & Support

Welcome to the Help & Support page of BookReviewer. We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have regarding our platform. Below are some common topics and answers to help you make the most of your experience:

Getting Started

How do I login to my account?

To login to your account, click on the "Log In" button located at the top right corner of the homepage. Enter your registered email address and password, then click "Log In."

What if I don’t have an account?

To create an account on Boo, click on the "Register" button located at the middle of the login. Fill in the required information, such as your email address, and password, and then click "Next"

Book Management

How do I add a book to my library?

To add a book to your library, search for the desired book using the search bar or press the „Books” page on the header. Once you've found the book, click on it to view the details. Then, click the "Add to Library" button to add it to your personal library.

How do I mark a book as "currently reading" or "read"?

Navigate to your library and locate the book you want to update. Click on the book to view its details, then select the appropriate status (e.g., "Currently Reading," "Read") from the options provided.

Reviews and Discussions

How do I write a book review?

To write a book review, navigate to the book's page in your library or search for it on the website. Scroll down to the review section and click on "Write a Review." Provide your rating, write your review, and click "Submit" to publish it.

Account Management

How do I update my profile information?

Click on your profile picture or username to access your profile settings. From there, you can update your name, email address, password, and other account details.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team at bookreviewer@email.com for assistance.

Contact Us

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at bookreviewer@email.com. We're here to help and ensure that you have a seamless experience on BookReviewer.
